Numerical Methods in Radiation Room Shielding

Researched methods of Monte Carlo numerical approximation of particle interactions to determine the shielding effectiveness of a treatment room in a radiation therapy clinic. Discussed practicality of these methods and their limitations based on hardware constraints and applicability to the problem at hand. Compiled into a poster for the PHYS 404 class of 2022 at UBC.
